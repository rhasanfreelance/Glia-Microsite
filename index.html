The idea

Neurons get the credit. Glial cells do the work — feeding, insulating, repairing, so the signal never drops. We built an AI in their image: support intelligence, not spotlight intelligence.

What it actually does

A few quiet jobs, done constantly.

OBSERVE

Reads context, not messages

GLIA follows what's actually happening across your calendar, threads and files — the situation, not just the words.

CONNECT

Wires the gaps between tools

It notices when a decision in one app should update another, and closes that loop before you'd think to.

ACT

Takes the small actions for you

Reschedules, drafts, files, flags — the two-minute tasks that quietly eat every afternoon, handled before you ask.

GOVERN

Shows its work, always

Every action GLIA takes is logged in plain language and reversible in one tap — nothing happens in a black box.
